About

I am a first-year M.S student at Algorithmic Intelligence Laboratory (ALIN-LAB), advised by Prof. Jinwoo Shin at Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (KAIST). I received my B.S. degrees in Electrical Engineering at KAIST in 2022.

Research

My primary research interests lie in the area of natural language processing (NLP) and machine learning (ML). The long-term goal of my research is to enhance the practicality of NLP systems (e.g., question answering) so that they can be widely used in real-world scenarios. In particular, my research focuses on how to i) make NLP models cheaper (i.e., efficient) in terms of data and computational resources, and ii) design robust/reliable ML algorithms to prevent undesirable outcomes in the wild. Here are some keywords:

  • Question Answering
  • Retrieval-Based NLP
  • Efficiency
  • Robustness
